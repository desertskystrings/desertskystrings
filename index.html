About Desert Sky Strings

Who we are

Desert Sky Strings is a new string ensemble in Valley of the Sun! We are currently building a Community Orchestra and a small performance ensemble! Our Community Orchestra is open to all string musicians 18 years and older with at least 1 year of playing experience. Yes, 50 years ago in high school counts! Dust off your instrument and join US!

Our small performance ensemble is rehearsing weekly and available for booking for live entertainment for private events! Currently we are building this ensemble as a string quartet but as we continue to develop as an organization we hope to open to other instruments!

Join our community orchestra!

Desert Sky Strings is currently holding open community orchestra rehearsals once a month and we hope to increase frequency as we continue to develop our organization to include facilities and support roles. The community orchestra is open to all musicians 18 years or older with at least one year of playing experience.

Desert Sky Strings is also holding weekly small ensemble rehearsals. We are currently seeking a Viola player for a primary seat and have understudy seats available as well for other positions. Due to the nature of this group, an informal audition is required to be offered a position in this ensemble.

Rehearsals are currently being held Mondays at 4:30 PM. Please contact us to request more information and join us in creating music and community!
